#  Skill 21 - Pi WebCam

Author: Nnenna Eze

Date: 2020-10-22
-----

## Summary
For skill 21, the pi camera was hooked up to the raspberry pi, and camera modules were installed. When the camera service is started, the ip address of the connected laptop can be typed into the web browser, with a port of 8081, and the video displayed by the camera will appear. Pictures can also be taken with the camera, and are saved to the file test.jpg on the raspberry pi
